Tempesta Gallery Website

A minimalistic, bilingual website for a contemporary art gallery in Milan

Designed a sleek, multilingual website that embodies Tempesta Gallery’s artistic identity. The layout highlights exhibitions and artists, with a focus on intuitive navigation for international visitors. Every design choice was crafted to reflect the gallery’s contemporary and refined vision, ensuring a seamless connection between the digital platform and the brand’s essence.

Contributor: Gorazd Guštin.
